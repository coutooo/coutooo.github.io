How to Play

Start Playing
Stack the Blocks
Clear Lines & Score

Use simple touch controls to move and rotate falling blocks. Position them strategically to create complete horizontal lines. When a line is completed, it disappears and you earn points. Keep clearing lines to prevent the stack from reaching the top!
